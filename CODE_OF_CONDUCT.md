# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors, users, and community members of the UI8Kit Framework project, regardless of background or identity. UI8Kit aims to foster a collaborative community focused on building accessible, semantic, and performant web interfaces.

## Expected Behavior

* Be respectful and considerate in all interactions
* Use welcoming and inclusive language
* Show empathy and kindness toward others
* Accept constructive feedback gracefully
* Focus on collaborative problem-solving
* Contribute to making UI8Kit a better framework for everyone

## Unacceptable Behavior

* Offensive, abusive, or discriminatory language
* Personal attacks or harassment
* Disruptive or inappropriate conduct
* Violation of professional standards
* Spam or off-topic content in discussions

## Enforcement

Violations will be addressed by project maintainers through appropriate corrective actions, including warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent bans. Reports can be made by contacting the project maintainers or opening an issue on GitHub.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces including GitHub repositories, discussions, documentation, and public representations of the UI8Kit community.
